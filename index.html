ABOUT
SMARTLAB

SmartLab is your all-in-one solution for managing laboratory operations with ease and confidence. Designed to simplify equipment borrowing and room scheduling, SmartLab lets users submit requests online while the system instantly checks availability and detects scheduling conflicts. Every request is clearly reviewed and approved by administrators, ensuring an organized and reliable process from start to finish.

Say goodbye to overlapping schedules, misplaced equipment, and time-consuming manual checks. With automated schedule validation and real-time inventory monitoring, SmartLab helps laboratories run smoothly, efficiently, and fairly. Built-in reporting and export tools make it easy for administrators to track usage, plan resources, and maintain accurate records all in just a few clicks.

Whether you’re a student reserving equipment, a faculty member scheduling a lab session, or an administrator overseeing operations, SmartLab gives you a smarter, faster, and more dependable way to manage your laboratory.
